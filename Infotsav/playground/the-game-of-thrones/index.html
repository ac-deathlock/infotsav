How to play

Goal:

Your goal is to bring Khaleesi (The player marked with the red flag) to the Iron Throne

Rules:

  • Travel over the board from square to square with any player you want by click it. Once you clicked it, you'll get the relevant movements options (Marked as gray squares).
  • You can only move forward, reverse, and side-to-side. No diagonal movement is allowed.
  • You can only move the players on the board if its movement can be blocked by another player.
  • A move is complete when you land on the board square next to the blocking player. They act like your brakes.
  • You win by getting Khaleesi to the Iron Throne (The block in the center).

That's it, you're all set!
